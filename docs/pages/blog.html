Chess

July 16, 2023

I feel like chess is a very interesting game because it's not only about moving pieces randomly and expecting to win. It depends on how you analyze the game, trying to understand what your opponent wants to do and how you strategically plan your moves on the board, reacting to your opponent, with the ultimate goal of targeting the king. There is zero luck involved in this game, which is why I like it so much. As Andrew Tate once tweeted and said, 'In chess, there is no luck. NONE. If you lose, somewhere - at some point, you made a mistake.
